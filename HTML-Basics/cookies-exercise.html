Let's add a recipte for some delicious chocolate chip cookies

We're going to borrow a nice picture from General Mills



Here is what the recipe calls for

    Ingredients
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups of floor
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 cup of sugar
    Instructions
  1. Add eggs, floor and vanilla extract and mix
  2. Combine with sugar and baling powder and stir
  3. Eat half the cookie batter because it is delicious
  4. Take the rest of the cookie batter and make cookies
  5. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes
  6. Enjoy
